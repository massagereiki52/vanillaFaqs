Easter FAQ's

What does Easter represent?

Easter represents the death and resurrection of Jesus.
Christians believe that he was man, and God all in one.

How long was Jesus' ministry?

Jesus' ministry was strong and fast growing.
His ministry was all but 3 years. According to biblical scollars the number three represents divine wholeness, completion, and perfection.

What do Christians mean when they say that they are called to be the hands and feet of Jesus?

This means that as a Christian we are called to live out our lives just as Jesus does. We are called to love our neighbors. even the one's that we may not get a long with, this can be the most difficult thing to do.

What does good friday represent?

Good Friday represents the death of Jesus on the cross. You see Jesus had to die for those who believe in Him to be saved from the pain, sorrow, and sins of living in this world.

What does worship mean?

What's the first thing that you think of when you think of the word worship? Most of you will probably think of music, but I'm here to tell you that worship is so much more than just the music. Worship is the life style that you lead, it is the tears that you cry, it is the very breath in your lungs. Worship is all of these things and so much more, because worship is infinite.
